Description

Join us for a dynamic three-day event packed with cutting-edge presentations, interactive workshops, and a lot of fun! Dive deep into the latest trends in AI, blockchain, cybersecurity, and more as industry leaders and visionary speakers share their insights and expertise. Whether you're a seasoned professional or a budding entrepreneur, NextGen offers a platform to expand your knowledge, forge meaningful connections, and ignite your passion for technology.

Date: October 15-17, 2024

Time: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Location: Convention Center, Gostivar, Macedonia




Three Day Schedule

Day 1: October 15, 2024

  • Morning: Keynote on Future Trends
  • Mid-morning: Panel on AI Innovations
  • Afternoon: Workshops on Blockchain and Cybersecurity
  • Late afternoon: Industry Talks on AI and Blockchain
  • Evening: Networking Reception

Day 2: October 16, 2024

  • Morning: Keynote on Cybersecurity Challenges
  • Mid-morning: Panel on Future of Work
  • Afternoon: Workshops on AI Ethics and Blockchain in Supply Chain
  • Late afternoon: Industry Talks on Cybersecurity and Regulatory Challenges
  • Evening: Networking Mixer

Day 3: October 17, 2024

  • Morning: Keynote on Machine Learning Advancements
  • Mid-morning: Panel on Impact of AI and Blockchain on Society
  • Afternoon: Workshops on Blockchain Security and AI for Social Good
  • Late afternoon: Industry Talks on AI in Healthcare and Blockchain Identity Management
  • Evening: Closing Ceremony and Networking Farewell



Keynote Speakers

John Smith

John Smith is the CEO of Tech Innovations Inc., a leading company in artificial intelligence research and development. With over two decades of experience in the tech industry, John is known for his groundbreaking work in machine learning and data analytics.

Emily Johnson

Emily Johnson is a renowned cybersecurity expert and the founder of CyberSafe Solutions. She has been instrumental in helping organizations strengthen their cybersecurity posture and defend against emerging threats.
